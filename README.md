Rock Paper Scissors (Player vs Computer)
This Rock Paper Scissors game lets you play against the computer by choosing stone, paper, or scissors. Both choices are revealed after a short animation, and the winner is decided based on classic game rules. Scores are tracked each round, and you can reset the game anytime to start over.

How to Play:
1. Click STONE, PAPER, or SCISSORS to make your choice.
2. The computer will automatically choose its option at the same time.
3. Both hands will animate, then reveal the selected choices.
4. The rules are:
   Stone beats Scissors
   Scissors beats Paper
   Paper beats Stone
5. If both choices are the same, the round is a Draw.
6. The winner of the round earns 1 point, shown on the scoreboard.
7. Click RESET GAME to restart the game and reset the scores.

Try your Luck and beat the computer!
